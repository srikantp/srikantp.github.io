    • Financial Market Microstructure : market design, price formation, empirical models
    • Fixed Income Securities : products, markets, interest rate modelling, pricing derivatives
    • Financial Instruments and Markets
  • 2 year MBA Economics courses ( 30 hours )
    • Econometrics : introduction to concepts, tools and approaches
    • Investment Banking services : overview of businesses; introduction to various activities; control challenges
    • Financial Management - II : Second mba core course in finance - arbitrage and derivatives; capital structure, dividend policy and valuation; practical financial management in the long and short term
  • 1 year MBA Economics courses ( 20 hours )
    • Financial Management - II : Second mba core course in finance - arbitrage and derivatives; capital structure, dividend policy and valuation; practical financial management in the long and short term
    • Game Theory Applications in Finance : introductory concepts; some models in finance
  • Undergraduate ( 40 hours )
    • Economics of networks : network analysis, models, games
